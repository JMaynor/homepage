# Review

A little one-note, but a nice novella. I guess I don't read novellas all that often. I suppose that probably is the intent of the thing? Find one simple idea, explore it fully, move on. Which does fit my MO. I think I just found myself interested with the captain of the Demeter and wanted to explore further.
