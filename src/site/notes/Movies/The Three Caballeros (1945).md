# Review

I do enjoy the slightly educational cartoons. This one was more just animation fun than [[Movies/Saludos Amigos (1942)\|Saludos Amigos]]. But they make the world feel like a bright and kind place. I'm not even the sorta person who's terrified of everything. But I'll admit, I like how these cartoons showcase places that I either don't think about much, or think of potential dangers that are there. And we just have a bright-eyed and optimistic view of things. I remember seeing so many of these multicultural exposes as a child. And at a point, we stop bothering to tell people of the lovely things outside of your neck of the woods. A real shame. But this was fun. I like Donald. I like José and Panchito. The combining live action and animation went well. If I have any complaints it was that we didn't really learn as much here than as in Saludos. But still a generally fun time was had.
