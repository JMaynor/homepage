# Review

I like when we learn about other cultures. It's fun! This one was different, obviously. Not quite one of the full animated movies and more just a collection of shorts. I mostly liked them. The planes I could take or leave. I enjoyed our officially introduction to Donald Duck. That's fun. Such humble beginnings. Crazy he starts here and ends up saving the world from the seekers of darkness. Good on him.

I think the last short where we learn how to samba was clearly the best. I could feel a little bit of that [[Movies/Fantasia (1940)\|Fantasia]] energy. Also enjoyed all the interstitial map bits. Love seeing the heavily stylized countries. Always feels nostalgic. Probably for the best we stayed in the Americas, given what year it is...
